Graphene - The Way of the Future

Graphene is a material that is 200 times stronger than steel. Comprised of carbon and only one atom thick, graphene is the most highly conductive material in the world. The trouble is, graphene is still in the early development stage and no one has yet discovered how to make a continuous conductor out of it.

Our new line of cables, The Graphene Extreme, is the embodiment of all of our composite development capabilities. While solid conductors of graphene do not yet exist, our expertise lies in combining exotic materials to maximize the strengths of those materials, while minimizing their weaknesses. We use the unique characteristics of graphene to supercharge other materials and create composite conductors that the world has never experienced before.
